1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Resume Generator application. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Data We Collect

Our app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help improve your experience. This includes:

  • Template Usage: Which resume templates are selected most frequently
  • User Interactions: How often users preview their CVs and generate PDFs
  • Performance Data: App loading times and responsiveness metrics
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and language settings for troubleshooting
  • Feature Usage: Which sections of the app are used most often

We Do NOT Collect:

  • Any personal information you enter in your resumes (names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.)
  • Contact details unless explicitly provided for support
  • Precise location data or GPS information
  • Payment information (our app is free with no in-app purchases)

3. How We Use Your Data

The anonymous data we collect helps us:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Identify and fix crashes and bugs
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Prioritize development of new templates and features
  • Optimize the user experience based on usage patterns
  • Make informed decisions about future updates

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Anonymous analytics data is processed by:

  • Firebase Analytics: Google's analytics service that helps us understand app usage
  • Crashlytics: For identifying and resolving app crashes (if implemented)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights & Choices

European Union (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

California, USA (CCPA/CPRA)

Under California Consumer Privacy Act, you have:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected
  • The right to delete personal information
  • The right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable data protection laws including:

  • Canada's PIPEDA
  • Australia's Privacy Act
  • Singapore's PDPA
  • Malaysia's PDPA
  • South Africa's POPI Act
