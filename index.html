1. Introduction

Welcome to Rezume CV Maker ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Key Points:

  • We use Google Sign-In for authentication
  • We store minimal user data in Firebase Firestore
  • We use Google AdMob for advertising
  • We comply with all applicable privacy laws
  • You have control over your data

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your display name (if available)
  • A unique user identifier from Google

We do NOT collect: Your Google password or any other sensitive account information.

2.2 Premium User Data

For premium users, we store in Firebase Firestore:

  • Your user ID (from Google authentication)
  • Premium status (true/false)
  • Subscription purchase date (if applicable)
  • Subscription expiration date (if applicable)
  • Transaction ID (for verification)

2.3 Resume Data

All resume/CV data you create is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your resume content on our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (future feature).

2.4 Advertising Data

Our app uses Google AdMob, which may collect:

  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • Advertising ID
  • General location (country/city level)
  • App usage data related to ads

AdMob data is used to:
