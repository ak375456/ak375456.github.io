1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in relation to advertising in our Resume Generator application. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Advertising in Our App

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads, which helps us keep the app free. Here's what you need to know:

2.1 Types of Ads Shown

  • Banner Ads: Small ads at the bottom of the screen
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown between CV edits
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional ads to unlock premium features

2.2 Data Used for Ads

AdMob may collect:

  • Device Information: OS version, device model
  • Advertising ID: Non-personal identifier for ad targeting
  • General Location: Country/city (not precise GPS)
  • App Usage Data: Interactions with ads

We do NOT collect: Your name, email, resume content, or exact location.

2.3 Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

Depending on your region and consent, you may see:

  • Personalized Ads: Tailored to your interests (higher revenue for us)
  • Non-Personalized Ads: Generic ads (lower revenue but privacy-focused)

2.4 Your Ad Choices by Region

European Union (GDPR)

We show a consent popup where you can:

  • Accept personalized ads (uses your Advertising ID)
  • Choose non-personalized ads (limited data usage)
  • Withdraw consent anytime in app settings

California, USA (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of "sale" of your data (via "Do Not Sell My Data" toggle in settings)
  • Request deletion of ad-related data

Canada (PIPEDA)

We provide:

  • Transparency about data collection
  • Ability to opt out of personalized ads

2.5 Opting Out of Ad Personalization

To disable personalized ads:

  1. Open Settings in our app
  2. "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (Android)
  3. Or email us at ak375456@gmail.com
Request Opt-Out
